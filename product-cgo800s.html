Crafted for comfort

Every detail designed with the urban commuter in mind.

The Frame

Hydroformed 6061 aluminum with an elegant step-through option. The battery integrates seamlessly into the downtube, keeping the center of gravity low and the aesthetics clean.

Comfort Geometry

An upright riding position keeps your back straight and your eyes on the road. The swept-back handlebar brings controls to your natural reach—no stretching, no strain.

Integrated Lighting

Front and rear lights are built into the frame, powered by the main battery. Auto-on at dusk ensures you're always visible—and always legal.
