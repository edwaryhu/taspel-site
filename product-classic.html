Award-winning design

Beautiful. Simple. Connected.

The minimalist aluminum frame flows seamlessly from stem to tail. The iconic integrated front light defines our signature look—visible from any angle. Every cable is hidden, every bolt recessed. Technology disappears into design, turning heads wherever you go.

Design that transcends

Every line, every curve, every component—crafted with intention.

The Frame

Hydroformed 6061 aluminum tubing creates the perfect balance of strength and weight. The single-tube design eliminates visual clutter while providing exceptional rigidity. Every weld is smooth, every joint seamless.

Integrated Lighting

The iconic front light isn't just functional—it's a signature. 180° visibility ensures you're seen from every angle. Auto-on at dusk, auto-off at dawn. The rear light pulses intelligently during braking.

Hidden Technology

All cables run internally. The display integrates flush with the stem. The battery slides invisibly into the downtube. GPS, accelerometer, and connectivity—all invisible, all working silently.

Color & Finish

Each color is powder-coated in four layers for depth and durability. Matte finishes resist fingerprints. Gloss accents catch the light. Choose from six carefully curated colorways.
